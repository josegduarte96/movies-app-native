# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Spanish-speaking casual movie fans browsing on mobile at home. They want to quickly see what's playing, what's popular, and what's worth watching — decisively, without friction. Primary context: couch, evening, half-attention, scrolling with one thumb.

## Product Purpose

A movie discovery app that surfaces Now Playing, Top Rated, and Popular films from TMDB. Success is a user finding something to watch in under 30 seconds of opening the app.

## Brand Personality

Clean, minimal, modern. The app gets out of the way — posters and information lead, chrome recedes. Not editorial, not theatrical, not playful.

## Anti-references

- Streaming apps (Netflix, HBO, Prime Video, Disney+) — too commercial, too branded, too busy
- The official TMDB or IMDB sites — cluttered, data-dump UI
- Generic dark-mode movie apps (dark blue + teal) — the category default to avoid

## Design Principles

- **Focused exploration.** Guide users to discover, don't overwhelm with choices. Three curated categories, not a grid of everything.
- **Content first.** Movie posters and information lead; chrome stays out of the way. Let the imagery breathe.
- **Effortless browsing.** Zero friction between opening the app and finding something worth watching. One thumb, no dead taps.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard good practices: semantic roles, accessible labels on interactive elements, sufficient color contrast, reduced motion support.
